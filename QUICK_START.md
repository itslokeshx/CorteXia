# CorteXia Quick Start Guide

Welcome to CorteXia! This guide will help you get started with your new personal life operating system.

## Navigation

### Main Sidebar
The left sidebar provides access to all major sections:

- **Home** - Dashboard with life state overview and signal constellation
- **Tasks** - Task management with priority and time tracking
- **Habits** - Habit tracking with streak visualization
- **Time** - Time analytics and productivity insights
- **Money** - Finance tracking and budget management
- **Study** - Learning session tracking and goal progress
- **Goals** - Goal architecture with milestones
- **AI Insights** - AI-powered insights and recommendations
- **Journal** - Reflective journaling with mood tracking

### Header Features

- **Logo** - Click to return to dashboard
- **Quick Add Bar** - Type anything to log data (e.g., "spent $45", "studied 2h", "went to gym")
- **Dark Mode Toggle** - Switch between light and dark themes
- **Settings** - Access preferences and account settings

## Getting Started

### 1. Set Your Life Intentions
Start by visiting the **Goals** page. Define what matters to you:
- Career goals (skills, projects, positions)
- Health goals (fitness, sleep, nutrition)
- Learning goals (courses, books, subjects)
- Financial goals (savings, investments, budgets)

### 2. Establish Your Habits
Go to **Habits** and create daily/weekly habits that support your goals:
- Meditation
- Exercise
- Learning time
- Journaling
- Reading

### 3. Track Your Time
Visit **Time** to understand where your hours go:
- Log focused work sessions
- Track meetings and admin time
- Monitor learning time
- Identify your peak hours

### 4. Log Your Tasks
Use the **Tasks** page to:
- Break goals into actionable tasks
- Prioritize high-impact work
- Estimate time requirements
- Track completion

### 5. Monitor Your Finances
Go to **Finance** to:
- Track income and expenses
- Set category budgets
- Identify spending patterns
- Plan adjustments

### 6. Record Study Sessions
Use **Study** to:
- Log learning time by subject
- Track focus quality
- Monitor progress toward learning goals
- Identify your best learning conditions

### 7. Journal Regularly
Visit **Journal** to:
- Reflect on your day
- Track mood and energy
- Identify patterns in your behavior
- Write freely without judgment

### 8. Review AI Insights
Check **AI Insights** regularly to:
- Discover behavioral patterns
- Get actionable recommendations
- Read your morning briefing
- Understand your life trends

### 9. Customize Your Settings
Adjust **Settings** to:
- Enable/disable notifications
- Control data sharing
- Set privacy preferences
- Customize appearance

## Core Concepts

### Life State
Your current life status calculated by AI across all domains:
- **High Momentum** - Everything aligned, strong progress
- **On Track** - Progressing well, sustainable pace
- **Drifting** - Some areas slipping, needs attention
- **Overloaded** - Too much, need to reduce scope

### Signal Constellation
Eight key signals monitoring different life dimensions:
- **Time** - Productivity and time allocation
- **Focus** - Deep work quality and concentration
- **Habits** - Consistency and habit completion
- **Goals** - Progress toward objectives
- **Money** - Financial health and spending patterns
- **Study** - Learning progress and focus
- **Screen** - Digital wellness and balance
- **Energy** - Overall energy levels and burnout risk

### AI Insights Categories

**Patterns** - Recurring behaviors and trends detected across your data

**Warnings** - Areas requiring attention (high screen time, broken habits, overspending)

**Opportunities** - Actionable improvements that could significantly impact your life

**Successes** - Celebrations of achievements and positive momentum

## Tips for Success

1. **Be Consistent** - Log data regularly for AI patterns to emerge
2. **Be Honest** - Accurate data leads to accurate insights
3. **Review Weekly** - Check insights every Sunday for synthesis
4. **Act on Recommendations** - Try suggested changes for 2-3 weeks
5. **Celebrate Wins** - Acknowledge progress in habits and goals
6. **Iterate** - Adjust your approach based on what works
7. **Protect Deep Work** - Use your identified peak hours wisely
8. **Rest Intentionally** - Balance is key to sustainability

## Example Workflows

### Monday Morning
1. Check AI morning briefing
2. Review weekly goals
3. Set up this week's tasks
4. Log upcoming study sessions
5. Check budget status

### Daily (Evening)
1. Log completed tasks
2. Log time spent across categories
3. Check off completed habits
4. Record quick journal entry
5. Review tomorrow's priorities

### Weekly (Sunday)
1. Read weekly synthesis in AI Insights
2. Review goal progress
3. Analyze spending patterns
4. Plan next week's study time
5. Reflect in journal

### Monthly (End of Month)
1. Review all goals and progress
2. Analyze spending categories and adjust budget
3. Celebrate wins and acknowledge challenges
4. Plan next month's focus areas
5. Check financial health
